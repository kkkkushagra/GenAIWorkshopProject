COLLEGE PLACEMENT POLICY

Placement Cell Guidelines

1. Eligibility

Students must have:
- Minimum 6.0 CGPA
- No active disciplinary cases
- Completed mandatory internships

2. Backlog Policy

Students with active backlogs are not eligible to sit for campus placements unless specifically permitted by the recruiting company.

3. Attendance Requirement

Students participating in placement drives must maintain at least 75% attendance.

4. Dream Company Policy

A dream company is defined as offering a package above ₹10 LPA.

Students placed in a dream company cannot participate in further placement drives.

5. Offer Acceptance

Students receiving an offer must confirm acceptance within three working days.

6. Dress Code

Formal dress is mandatory during placement drives.

7. Resume Verification

Providing false information in resumes may result in disqualification.

8. Internship Policy

Students securing internships through the placement cell must complete the internship successfully.

9. Placement Training

Students are required to attend aptitude, coding, communication, and interview preparation sessions.
