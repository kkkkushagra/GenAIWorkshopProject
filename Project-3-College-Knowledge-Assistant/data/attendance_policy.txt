COLLEGE ATTENDANCE POLICY

Effective From: July 2025

1. Minimum Attendance

Students must maintain at least 75% attendance in every course.

2. Attendance Calculation

Attendance is calculated separately for each subject.

3. Medical Leave

Students with genuine medical emergencies may apply for attendance relaxation by submitting medical certificates within seven days.

Maximum relaxation allowed is 10%.

4. Shortage of Attendance

Students having attendance below 75% without valid approval will not be allowed to appear in End Semester Examinations.

5. Late Entry

Students arriving more than 15 minutes late may be marked absent for that lecture.

6. Proxy Attendance

Proxy attendance is considered misconduct and disciplinary action may be taken.

7. Extra Classes

Attendance in remedial and extra classes will also be considered.

8. Attendance Portal

Students can monitor attendance through the college ERP portal.

9. Faculty Responsibility

Faculty members must update attendance within 24 hours after every lecture.

10. Holiday Adjustment

Government holidays are excluded from attendance calculation.
