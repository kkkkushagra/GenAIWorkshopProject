COLLEGE ACADEMIC HANDBOOK

Academic Year: 2025-2026

1. Semester Structure
The college follows a semester system with two semesters in one academic year.

2. Credit System
Each course carries credits based on lecture, tutorial, and laboratory hours.

3. Attendance Requirement
Students must maintain at least 75% attendance in every subject to appear in the End Semester Examination.

4. Backlog Policy
Students having backlogs may register for supplementary examinations as per university regulations.

5. Grading System

O Grade : 90-100
A+ Grade : 80-89
A Grade : 70-79
B+ Grade : 60-69
B Grade : 50-59
C Grade : 40-49
F Grade : Below 40

6. Examination Rules
Students must carry their college ID card during examinations.

Electronic gadgets including mobile phones and smart watches are strictly prohibited.

7. Revaluation
Students may apply for answer sheet revaluation within 10 days after result declaration.

8. Project Evaluation
Final year projects are evaluated based on:
- Innovation
- Implementation
- Presentation
- Documentation

9. Academic Integrity
Cheating, plagiarism, or unfair means during examinations may result in disciplinary action.

10. Maximum Credits
A student can register for a maximum of 28 credits in one semester with department approval.

11. Library Rules
Books can be borrowed for 14 days.
Late submission incurs a fine of ₹5 per day.

12. Academic Calendar
Mid Semester Examination: September
End Semester Examination: December
