COLLEGE HOSTEL RULES

1. Hostel Timings

Hostel gates close at 9:30 PM.

Students returning after this time require prior permission from the hostel warden.

2. Visitor Policy

Visitors are allowed only between 4:00 PM and 7:00 PM.

3. Leave Policy

Students must submit leave applications through the hostel office.

Parents may be contacted for overnight leave.

4. Mess Timings

Breakfast:
7:30 AM - 9:00 AM

Lunch:
12:30 PM - 2:00 PM

Snacks:
5:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Dinner:
7:30 PM - 9:00 PM

5. Hostel Discipline

Ragging is strictly prohibited.

Consumption of alcohol, smoking, and drugs inside the hostel is prohibited.

6. Room Inspection

Wardens may inspect rooms periodically.

7. Electrical Appliances

Induction cookers, heaters, and electric kettles are not allowed without permission.

8. Internet

Wi-Fi is available from 6:00 AM to 12:00 Midnight.

9. Complaint Register

Students can report maintenance issues through the hostel office or ERP portal.

10. Fine Policy

Damage to hostel property will result in recovery charges and disciplinary action.
